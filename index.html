About

I'm Ahmed Mohamed Abdel Rahim, a GIS Analyst, GIS Solutions Specialist and Web Developer with over 6 years of experience delivering innovative geospatial solutions and dynamic web applications for both government and private sector clients. I specialize in spatial data analysis, interactive mapping, asset inventory management, and developing user-friendly web interfaces. Proficient in ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, and Python scripting, I automate complex GIS workflows and produce high-quality maps, dashboards, and spatial analyses. Additionally, I bring strong frontend development skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS, enabling me to create responsive and intuitive web applications tailored to client needs. Currently, I work as a GIS Analyst, Cartographer and GIS Solutions Specialist at Alqotr Company in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, where I manage and develop geospatial solutions, design high-quality maps, perform spatial analysis, and automate GIS workflows using Python. I also oversee ArcGIS Online platforms, create interactive dashboards and mapping applications, and provide technical support and training for GIS projects. Holding a Bachelor's degree in Geography from Cairo University, I have also Esri certifications earned several including “Spatial Analysis with ArcGIS Pro,” “Deploying and Maintaining a Multiuser Geodatabase,” and Python for ArcGIS. I'm passionate about integrating emerging technologies to optimize geospatial operations and develop impactful, data-driven web solutions.
